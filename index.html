About Me

I am a storyteller dedicated to creating compelling narratives through film and sound. With an eye for detail and a passion for creating emotion, I believe in the power of visual and aural to inspire and connect us to our surroundings. My work includes everything from narratives to documentaries and commercial content. I am always looking for new ways to collaborate with others in order to bring important stories to life, especially if they involve spaceships. I like spaceships.

You can find more of my work and content on my YouTube channel: youtube.com/benhasglasses.

Follow me on **Bluesky**: @benhasglasses.
